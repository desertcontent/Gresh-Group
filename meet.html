The Gresh Group, Inc. is a Las Vegas-based Government and Media Affairs firm. As a former television news anchor and reporter, Bryan Gresh began working on state and Federal political campaigns which eventually led to a presence in the hallways and offices of the Legislative Building in Carson City. Government Affairs has since become the cornerstone of the company. Each client is the recipient of a determined work ethic augmented by professional communication skills.
